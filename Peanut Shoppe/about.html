Reviews

Jeff F.

This place is classic! Although they do serve many different variations of peanuts (roasted, spicy, chocolate, etc.), I went with a friend and got a snow cone and some chocolate malt candy(it's basically a candy shop). The feel of this place makes you feel like you're in the 50's/60's and I'm not sure the it's completely intentional. A neat place if you suddenly get a sweet tooth and are in the area. Give it a shot!

Chris M.

Old school candy slash roasted nuts shop. Fun to stop in and you can get some good mixed nuts.

Hayes H.

We decided to get a snow cone during this hot summer day with our daughter; she's one, and she absolutely loved her blue bubblegum snow cone. The Peanut Shoppe is a neat little place- we got a pound of Spanish peanuts which are delicious. We just love the snow cones. I had a peach one. They're not like Hawaiian Shaved Ice- this ice is harder and thicker shavings, but it's good. My wife let me know that her mother used to get snow cones here when she was a little girl, so this really is an old place with a personality. The three of us got snow cones and it was only $5 and some change. Totally awesome!
